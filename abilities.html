Extra abilities

Strengths & Weaknesses

In some enemies we find certain vulnerabilities or immunities, these types of enemy abilities are based on conditionalities:

  • Vulnerability/immunity during its attack
  • Vulnerability/immunity dependent on player's attack orientation

These types of additional constraints are used to increase or decrease the difficulty, as well as to force the player to opt for different attack strategies.

Connection abilities

There are some direct synergies between certain enemies, which when in close proximity to each other have an additional effect on their original mechanics.

Additional actions upon death

When killed, some enemies have a residual damage ability. The most common are explode on death or scatter projectiles. This type of dying ability is common in many roguelikes and gives a lot of strategic richness to the same enemy. It is a mechanic that makes the player have to be more careful while attacking and keeping their distance, as an enemy with this type of ability dying nearby could cause damage with virtually no response time. It is an enemy type that helps us gain space.

Other enemies have a utility ability on death rather than a damage ability, such as the ability to revive after a period of time or spawn another enemy.
